Dapp A — Purchaser

Buy a privacy-preserving voucher with tUSDC. Per voucher we generate a fresh secret key (kept off-chain), prove the create circuit in a worker, approve the tUSDC transfer, and call buyAndCreate on the pool. The dapp emits a deep link to the chat dapp carrying the note — that's how you "hold" the voucher.

This is the only dapp that needs the buyer's wallet — assigns and redeems route through the relay. In demo mode (URL has ?demoKey=…) a local key is used; otherwise you connect a real EIP-6963 wallet (MetaMask).
