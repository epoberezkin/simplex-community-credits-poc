Dapp C — Relay Operator

You are the paymaster relay. Users and community admins prove transactions in their chat dapp (no signer) and hand you the proof. You sign + submit the on-chain tx, paying DOT for gas. Redeems credit the operator with tUSDC, which is how the relay covers its costs.

In a real deployment this would all be automated — relays would auto-pull pending proofs from their users via SimpleX and submit without any UI. Here you click Submit manually so you can see what's happening.

Wallet

In demo mode (URL has ?demoKey=…) a local key is used. Otherwise connect any EIP-6963 wallet (MetaMask, etc.) — you'll need the configured chain registered and some DOT for gas.

Not connected

Pending submissions

Each entry is a fully proved assign or redeem ready to land on chain. You don't see the underlying note material — only enough to submit. Paste a deep link from chat, scan a QR, or open the link directly (preserves your ?demoKey=…).

Balances

DOT = your gas balance (decreases when you submit). tUSDC = your free balance (increases on withdraw). Credit = tUSDC the pool owes you from redeems you relayed; click withdraw to settle.
